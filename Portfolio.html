About Me

Hi, I am Harry Potter, a wizard and student at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. I'm known as "The Boy Who Lived" after surviving an attack from the dark wizard Voldemort when I was just a baby.

I'm currently in Gryffindor House and have been on many magical adventures with my best friends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger. When I'm not studying spells or playing Quidditch, I enjoy protecting Hogwarts from dark forces.
