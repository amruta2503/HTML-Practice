AI-Powered Search Engine Launched by ByteTech

ByteTech has launched a new AI-powered search engine named NeuronFind, promising faster and smarter results than existing platforms. The launch event took place in San Francisco and was attended by over 500 industry experts. Reported by: Sneha Kapoor

Cybersecurity Breach Affects Major Bank

A major cybersecurity breach has impacted Global Trust Bank, exposing sensitive data of over 1.2 million customers. The bank has urged customers to monitor their accounts and has partnered with SecureWave for damage control. Warning: Avoid clicking on suspicious links if you’re a customer. Updated on: July 15, 2025

Electric Vehicles Sales Surpass Petrol Cars in Europe

For the first time, electric vehicle (EV) sales have outpaced petrol car sales in Europe. Analysts say this marks a turning point in the continent’s transition toward sustainable transportation. Germany, Norway, and Netherlands led the EV market growth. Author: Amar Singh
